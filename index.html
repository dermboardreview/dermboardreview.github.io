Description

Dermatology Board Review Q & A, released on April 28, 2011, is an app that targets dermatologists in training. It contains 3100 practice questions for the dermatology board examination. The questions were written using material in major dermatology textbooks. While the majority of the questions are in a written format, there are also several photo questions. The questions are challenging and are sure to test the user's knowledge, and the questions are short answer style questions. Anyone who is preparing for the board certification exam in dermatology is sure to find this app very helpful.

About Scott Jackson MD

Scott M. Jackson, MD, FAAD is a board-certified dermatologist in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and clinical associate professor with the Department of Dermatology, LSU Health Sciences Center, New Orleans. He completed his residency in 2007 and was named the 2007 Resident of the Year by the Medical Center of Louisiana (New Orleans). A member of Alpha Omega Alpha, Scott has published one book, which is titled Differential Diagnosis for the Dermatologist. The Dermatology Board Review Q & A app is his second major publication.
